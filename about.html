About

Roy Tharakan

A Journey of Leadership and Transformation

Empowering Leaders, Transforming Lives

Roy Tharakan is a distinguished leader and coach with over 27 years of experience in the B2B food ingredients industry across Asia Pacific, the Middle East, and Africa. Currently serving as the Director of the Global Commercial Talent Academy, Roy has dedicated his career to empowering professionals and executives to achieve their highest potential.

Roy's journey is marked by his deep industry knowledge and a passion for leadership development. His coaching approach combines strategic insight with personal growth, helping individuals navigate their careers and achieve transformative results. Committed to excellence, Roy's mission is to inspire and guide you toward unparalleled success and fulfillment.

Roy Tharakan 27+ Years of Services
Technical Trainee, Britannia Industries Limited (1996 - 1997)

Roy started his career as a technical trainee, contributing to the production scale-up in the baked goods sector. This early experience laid the foundation for his future roles in leadership, coaching, and team development.

Regional Manager, International Flavours & Fragrances Ltd. (IFF) (1997 - 2006)

From 1997 to 2006, Roy oversaw the flavour business across regions, developing and implementing strategies that drove growth. He dedicated himself to mentoring and guiding his team to achieve their professional goals.

Asst. Vice President, Food Specialities Limited (2006 - 2012)

From 2006 to 2012, as head of a food ingredient unit, Roy led a team, drove business development, and maintained strong customer relationships. His leadership emphasized motivation, accountability, and a successful culture.

Sales Director - Gulf Region, Kerry Ingredients & Flavours (2012 - 2014)

From 2012 to 2014, Roy was instrumental in establishing Kerry's presence in the Middle East. He built and led a high-performing team, providing continuous mentorship that enabled his staff to grow and succeed in a rapidly evolving market.

Director - Go-to-Market, Food Ingredients Asia Pacific, Cargill (2016 - 2021)

From 2016 to 2021, Roy was responsible for developing and executing market strategies across the Asia Pacific. He played a crucial role in mentoring his teams, ensuring they were equipped with the skills and mindset needed to excel in a highly competitive industry.

Regional Director - Commercial Excellence, Cargill (2021 - 2023)

From 2021 to 2023, Roy led the transformation of commercial excellence in the Asia Pacific region, driving customer-centric growth by investing in people. His leadership involved coaching cross-functional teams to embrace change and deliver outstanding results.

Director - Global Commercial Talent Academy, Cargill (2023 - Present)

In his current role from 2023 to present, Roy focuses on mentoring and coaching a diverse global team, helping them realize their potential and thrive in their careers. He takes pride in seeing his colleagues expand into new roles, leveraging the skills and confidence gained through his guidance.
