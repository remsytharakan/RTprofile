About

Roy Tharakan

A Journey of Leadership and Transformation

Empowering Leaders, Transforming Lives

Roy Tharakan is a distinguished leader and coach with over 27 years of experience in the B2B food ingredients industry across Asia Pacific, the Middle East, and Africa. Currently serving as the Director of the Global Commercial Talent Academy, Roy has dedicated his career to empowering professionals and executives to achieve their highest potential.

Roy's journey is marked by his deep industry knowledge and a passion for leadership development. His coaching approach combines strategic insight with personal growth, helping individuals navigate their careers and achieve transformative results. Committed to excellence, Roy's mission is to inspire and guide you toward unparalleled success and fulfillment.
